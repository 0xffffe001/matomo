Here you can manage various GDPR rights. In case you do not know what GDPR is, have a look at the Overview page.

In case you are searching for example a data subject by Visit IP to exercise the right of erasure or right to be forgotten, you may want to include additional visits of that visitor by clicking on the visitorId. The IP of a visitor typically changes on many visits and therefore you would not find all visits.

If you are trying to export the data for a data subject by IP address please be aware that the same IP address may be assigned to different visitors and is therefore not really recommended to be used as a filter as you might be sending personal data from a different data subject to another data subject. Always make sure the visitor who wants to exercise the right of access is actually the visitor he claims to be. In most cases, the only reliable source for this is using the UserId feature
